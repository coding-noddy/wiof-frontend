Privacy Policy

The World is One Family is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of confidential information to the extent possible, subject to provisions of state and federal law. In particular, we do not re-distribute or sell personal information collected on our web servers.

Information collected:

Cookies

World is One Family site may use "cookies" in order to deliver web content specific to individual users' interests or to keep track of online purchasing transactions. Sensitive personal information is not stored within cookies.

This information is used to improve Web presentation and utilization. IP address information may also be used for troubleshooting purposes.

Distribution of collected information:

  • World is One Family site will not distribute or sell personal information to third-party organizations.
