Our Purpose

An ancient Indian idea of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam (The entire world is one family) is the need of the hour for this planet as we battle multiple challenges ranging from Climate Change, Global Warming, Pollution, Poverty, Hunger, Floods, Famines, Inequality, Pandemics, Riots, Mental Health Issues, Hyper-consumerism, Unemployment etc. The idea of well informed interconnected people sharing knowledge and helping each other can help us face these challenges in a much robust manner. Nowhere was it more evident than in these unprecedented challenging times of the Covid-19 crisis where people are staying isolated for several weeks at a stretch, away from the families, friends, communities and lifestyles that they are used to. Virtual connects are a blessing during such times but can't be just limited to socialization. We need meaningful connect with relevant information sharing pertaining to the planet so that we can leave it in a better one than we found it.

What we then need is a deeper introspection about the kind of lives that we had led over the years in the mad rat race of life and the elusive search for money, fame, power which we think will bring us happiness, but which has only brought us frustration and despondency with a lack of fulfillment in general with our lives. We not only need to heal ourselves by self care such that we feel more in harmony with the universe, but also need to care about the ecosystem and the biodiversity all around us, which is being decimated with every passing day at an alarming rate. And armed with the knowledge that we acquire in this quest, we need to disseminate it to a wider network by having deeper meaningful connections.

It is with this idea, that we few like minded people, got together at the height of the Coronavirus Pandemic to start this Website WIOF (World Is One Family) which will combine the 3 major aspects of Creativity, Knowledge and Action (as exemplified in our slogan : Inspire through Creativity, Enlighten through Knowledge and Protect through Action) to create a portal that can lead a wider community of well-informed compassionate people helping each other to bring betterment to the society and the planet at large.

We have created the website painstakingly, over many weekends of this lock-down with one and only one purpose, to awaken, inform and encourage people to come together to make a difference to their lives and the lives of many others less fortunate so as to add meaning to their lives. So, urge you all to please support us in this endeavor and embark with us in this exciting journey of healing the universe. Just take the first step and we promise you that it will be the ride of your lives.

So, Welcome to the Family
