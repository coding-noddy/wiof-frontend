Why We Exist

An ancient Indian idea of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam (The entire world is one family) is the need of the hour as we battle multiple challenges — Climate Change, Pollution, Poverty, Mental Health, and more. Well-informed, interconnected people sharing knowledge can face these challenges together. This was evident during the Covid-19 crisis where meaningful virtual connections became essential.

🧘

Our Philosophy

We need deeper introspection about our lives — moving beyond the rat race toward harmony with the universe. We need to heal ourselves through self-care, protect the ecosystem and biodiversity around us, and share the knowledge we acquire to build deeper, meaningful connections with a wider community.

💡

How We Started

A few like-minded people came together during the Coronavirus Pandemic to start WIOF — combining three aspects: Creativity, Knowledge, and Action. Our slogan captures it: Inspire through Creativity, Enlighten through Knowledge, Protect through Action.

🤝

Join the Family

We built this website to awaken, inform, and encourage people to come together — to make a difference in their lives and the lives of those less fortunate. Take the first step and embark with us on this exciting journey of healing the universe.
